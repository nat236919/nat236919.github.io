About

My name is Nuttaphat Arunoprayoch (Chiang)

I am a software engineer and a data analyst who is gliding around open-source development.

I have experiences in using Python for various projects and applications ranging from its web frameworks, data manipulation, and data visualization. Additionally, I also have a fair experience developing and maintaining web-app projects using PHP and JS.

I am also no stranger with a variety of databases (e.g., MySQL, Azure Cosmos DB, and MongoDB). If the front-end layer is needed, I will not be hesitant to use HTML, CSS, JS, jQuery, and its frameworks (e.g., Bootstrap, Bulma, etc.) to get the job done.

Currently, I am working in a private R&D sector specialising in educational technology.
